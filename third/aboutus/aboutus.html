About us

TO KNOW ABOUT OUR LUXURY STORY


In 2004, the first Hotel HRM property opened its doors across from the Fabulous Fox Theatre in the eclectic arts and entertainment district of Midtown Atlanta. Today, we call culturally rich communities all over the world home, from New York to Paris, London to Shanghai, Hong Kong to Singapore—all the places you’ve always wanted to visit and do business in. Our team members go out of their way to make each stay memorable and our neighborhoods easy to discover and appreciate. From must-see, hear and eat experiences to those hidden gems off the beaten path, you can trust our team members to provide you with great local recommendations. Our properties feature unique design at every turn without compromising guest comfort. A vibrant modern color palette, complemented by natural light and locally influenced design and decor, make for an inviting, stylish and refreshing atmosphere.
